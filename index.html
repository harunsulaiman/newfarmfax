FAQ

What Financing models do you offer?

We offer input financing, feed loans, equipment financing, and flexible working capital — all tailored to agricultural seasons with repayment aligned to your harvest.

Can I apply?

Yes! Any registered farmer or agribusiness operating in Nigeria can apply through our fully digital platform.

How flexible is the loan?

Extremely flexible — no hidden fees, grace periods available, and repayment schedules are built around your harvest cycle.

How Fast is your approval rate?

Many farmers receive instant pre-approval. Full verification and funding typically happen within 24 hours.
